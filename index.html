🎓 About Me

Hello! I am Pooja Tiwari, a passionate mentor dedicated to guiding students toward academic and personal success. After completing my Master's degree in English Literature from the prestigious Banaras Hindu University (BHU), I discovered my true calling in teaching and mentoring young minds.

With a strong background in languages and humanities, I specialize in helping students develop excellent communication skills, critical thinking abilities, and a deep appreciation for literature and the arts. I believe in a student-centric approach, focusing not just on marks but on building confidence, creativity, and lifelong learning habits.

My mission is to create an inspiring and supportive learning environment where students feel empowered to express themselves and achieve their fullest potential.

📚 Courses Offered

I offer customized tutoring in the following subjects for students from grade 6 to undergraduate level:

  • 📖 English Language and Literature – Grammar, Comprehension, Writing Skills, Literary Analysis
  • 🧠 Personality Development – Communication Skills, Public Speaking, Confidence Building
  • 📝 Essay Writing & Creative Writing – Storytelling, Argumentative Essays, Academic Writing

Course Fees: Starting from ₹1,500/month for school students and ₹3,000/month for college students.

Each course is personalized according to the student’s learning goals, with flexible schedules to match individual needs. Special workshops and crash courses are also available on request.
